MOUNTAIN TREKKING & HIKING

Kilimanjaro mountain is the highest pick in Africa, spread to a total area of 1,688 square kilometers wide and stands at 5896m up to the sky. Amazingly it is the only snowing mountain in equator region, where the area is well marked as warming area on earth. But also, is the most visited mountain in the whole African continent. Rather Tanzania has other beautiful and attractive mountains to trek, as Mountain Meru and Oldoinyo Lengai in the northern circuit, and we have Uluguru mountains, Usambara mountains, Udzungwa Mountains and Mahale Mountains.
