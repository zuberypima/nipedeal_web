ZANZIBAR & COSTAL BEACHES

The Largest Island in Tanzania is Zanzibar among other, the island is allocated on the eastern coast of Tanzania, on the Indian Ocean, the island is full of beautiful white sand beaches, warm water. The Island is covered with different cultural heritage but with a unique Swahili culture & beautiful history tales. Zanzibar is made up of two majors islands, which are Unguja and Pemba. Tanzania is also blessed with more islands like Mafia in the Indian Ocean, Rubondo and other islands in Mainland, but also with more fine beaches along its coastal areas.
