PARKS & GAME RESERVES SAFARIS

Tanzania landscapes provides with most beautiful, attractive and spread with vast of natural plantation with different kind of trees and bushes, savanna land and water bodies, thus it tends to be attractive and supportive to a variety of different kind of small to big animals. Thus, as a country, Tanzania has many popular destinations but few to mention; National Parks like Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Manyara, Tarangire, Mikumi, Nyerere National Park, Arusha Park, Gombe Stream, Ruaha, Kitulo, Katavi, Saadani, Mkomazi, and Jozani forest and Selous Game reserves in southern Tanzania is the largest among other 21 game reserves.
