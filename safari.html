WHY MAKE YOUR SAFARI AND TREK IN TANZANIA

Tanzania is largest country in East Africa, full will unique natural and landscape as well as vast proportion of species and habitats in an unspoiled, peaceful environment all over the country.

In Tanzania you will interact with nature while participating in adventurous excursions to summit Africa's highest mountain (Mount Kilimanjaro) and the world famous Serengeti National Park (with amazing animal migration), finest beaches and historical preservation at its costal area and Zanzibar islands.

General in Tanzania terrain includes coastal plains, a central plateau, a highland in the north and south. Apart from Mountain Kilimanjaro, Tanzania has the second largest lake in the world (Lake Victoria) and the deepest Lake Tanganyika and vast of historical cultures.
